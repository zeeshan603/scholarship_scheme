Nabiyas Khan Memorial Scholarship

Those who knew our Abba closely must be aware of how deeply he was connected to the cause of education. He always used to say,

"I have a strong liking for teaching and reading, and I especially have an affection for children who cannot afford education."

This is a new beginning — a small effort to carry forward his wishes.

Scholarship Details

The aim is to encourage deserving students from selected schools. Every year, such students will be honored in their respective schools. They will receive a certificate and a cash prize as a token of appreciation.

1. S.A. Memorial Inter College Barauna

  • Students scoring 92% and above will get ₹4000.
  • Others in the top rank will get ₹3000.

2. Nursery School

  • Selected students from the school will get ₹2000.

3. Enfant India International School

  • Selected students from the school will get ₹2000.

Additional Rewards

  • Any student from our area who gets selected in a government engineering college will receive a reward of ₹5000.
  • Students scoring more than 85% in board exams may be considered for next year's tuition fee support.
  • Students who are selected in civil services will also be honored and encouraged. (The reward amount will be decided accordingly.)
For more details, contact: 9920454860
The Scholarship Award Ceremony will be held on 15th August every year — a special day to honor our achievers and inspire future generations.
Prize Distribution: Each recipient will receive a certificate of excellence along with their cash prize in a formal ceremony conducted at their respective schools. This ceremony is designed to celebrate their hard work, dedication, and encourage others to strive for academic success.
